Video and Display Setup

This matters more than most people think. The original Xbox does not output HDMI natively. Every HDMI solution involves signal conversion. What you are connecting to determines what that looks like.

Does your TV have composite or component inputs?
If your TV is HDMI-only, you will need an HDMI adapter. That changes the cost and the conversation.
Networking Setup

The original Xbox uses Ethernet only. No Wi-Fi, ever. Networking is also the most common source of problems — not because the Xbox is broken, but because modern equipment was not designed with 2001 consoles in mind. Be honest here.

How are you planning to connect the Xbox to your network?
What is your router situation?
ISP-provided combo units are a known problem for Insignia and Xbox networking in general. Not always fatal, but I need to know up front.
Are you comfortable logging into your router's admin settings if needed?
Do you plan to use Insignia for online play?
