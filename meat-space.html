Meat Man's Blurb

$$$WELCOME TO THE MEAT LOCKER$$$

Yo, I'm Meat Man. I got into modding through the GameBoy Zero project, then moved into emulation and Raspberry Pi builds, then PS3, then 360, and eventually landed on the original Xbox — which is where I stayed.

I have been modding original Xbox hardware for over two years. I have experience across multiple softmod methods, hardmod installs, firmware, dashboards, EEPROM recovery, and the full range of storage upgrades. Over 25 consoles re-homed and living online right now.

What makes me different from most people in this space: I am a gamer first. I actually use this hardware. I run a full analog component setup into a CRT, an HDMI setup, and a composite setup. I know what these machines look like at every output level because I use all of them.

I am not doing this for profit. That means I have no reason to oversell you, upsell you, or tell you something is necessary when it is not. If you do not need a modchip, I will tell you. If your setup plan is going to cause you problems, I will tell you that too. Before you spend money.

Do not confuse "beat up" with "bad." Some of the best machines look like they crawled out of a trunk.

Who I'd like to meet:
Gamers who actually intend to use the hardware. People who read the tour before messaging me. Anyone who respects a machine with scars and understands what it took to earn them.
